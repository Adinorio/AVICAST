Avicast Help & Support

Getting Started

Welcome to Avicast! This section will help you get started with the application.

  • Login: Use your registered username and password to log in.
  • Dashboard: The dashboard provides an overview of key features and quick access to reports.
  • Navigation: Use the sidebar menu to navigate through different sections of the application.

Birds List

The Birds List section allows you to view and manage a list of bird species.

  • Viewing Birds: Click on "Birds List" in the sidebar to view the list.
  • Searching Birds: Use the search functionality to find specific bird species.

Birds Identification

The Birds Identification section helps you identify bird species.

  • Uploading Images: Upload images of birds for identification.
  • Viewing Results: View the identification results and related information.

Sites

The Sites section allows you to manage water bird census reports.

  • Viewing Sites: Click on "Sites" in the sidebar to view the list of sites.
  • Adding Sites: Add new sites for census management.

Statistical Reports

The Statistical Reports section provides access to various reports and data visualizations.

  • Viewing Reports: Click on "Statistical Reports" to view available reports.
  • Filtering Data: Use filters to customize the data displayed in reports.
